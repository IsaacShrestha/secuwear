Technology used:
d3js
dcjs
crossfilter

Data source: 
events1.csv
#contains 14,00,000 events with timestamp, event-id, event, event-type, code-reference and domains

Note: This visualization includes
1) Donut chart: to display quantity of domain
2) Bar chart: to display quantity of events as per domain
3) Move chart: to display selectable events on the basis of timestamp
4) volume chart: to display volume of event as per selected event
5) table: to display list of 25 events as per selection

The timestamp is based on month which needs to be changed on milliseconds
